Led by Dr. Deepak Puthal | United Arab Emirates University (UAEU)

Technical Overview

The MAYA Research Group is a specialized laboratory dedicated to solving the "Resilience Gap" in modern computing. It focuses on the engineering of systems that are not only secure at rest but are capable of dynamic adaptation—altering their own configurations and security protocols in real-time to survive sophisticated attacks.

"To engineer autonomous, intelligence-driven systems that integrate security into the foundational layers of distributed computing, ensuring mission continuity and data integrity through adaptive resilience."

Core Research Pillars

1. Machine Intelligence for Adaptive Defense

Transitioning from static signatures to behavioral-based AI monitoring for autonomous self-healing and reinforcement-learning based defense optimization.

2. Secure Distributed Systems & Edge Computing

Solving security challenges in decentralized networks through Blockchain integration and optimized Edge-AI models for trillion-node IoT ecosystems.

3. Adversarial AI & Cyber-Deception

Focusing on active defense via simulated high-fidelity decoy environments and protecting neural networks from data poisoning attacks.
