Technical Overview

The MAYA Research Group is a specialized laboratory dedicated to solving the "Resilience Gap" in modern computing. It focuses on the engineering of systems that are not only secure at rest but are capable of dynamic adaptation—altering their own configurations and security protocols in real-time to survive sophisticated, multi-stage cyberattacks.

Core Research Pillars

Adaptive Defense

Developing Behavioral AI that monitors system telemetry to identify anomalies and trigger autonomous self-healing protocols.

Distributed Systems

Addressing security in decentralized networks through Blockchain integration and optimized Edge-AI models for IoT.

Cyber-Deception

Engineering high-fidelity decoy environments using Generative AI to divert and analyze adversarial behavior.

Director: Dr. Deepak Puthal

Dr. Deepak Puthal's research methodology emphasizes Computational Scalability. The lab’s work ensures that security measures do not become a bottleneck for system performance. By bridging the gap between high-level AI algorithms and low-level network protocols, the MAYA group produces "Systems-Aware AI."
