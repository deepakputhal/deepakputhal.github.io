Technical Overview

The MAYA Research Group focuses on AI-driven cybersecurity and resilient system design, addressing the challenges of dynamic, adversarial, and large-scale digital ecosystems.

“To engineer autonomous, intelligence-driven systems that embed security into the foundations of distributed computing.”

Core Research Pillars

Machine Intelligence for Adaptive Defense

Autonomous detection, reinforcement learning–based defense, and self-healing systems.

Secure Distributed Systems & Edge Computing

Blockchain-enabled trust, secure IoT, and scalable Edge-AI architectures.

Adversarial AI & Cyber-Deception

Adversarial learning defense, deception technologies, and robust AI pipelines.

Group Leadership

Dr. Deepak Puthal

Director and Founder of MAYA. His research bridges AI algorithms with system-level security, enabling scalable and deployable intelligent defense mechanisms.
